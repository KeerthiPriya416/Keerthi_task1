About Kanipakam

Kanipakam is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, located in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, India. The temple is known for its beautiful architecture, historical significance, and spiritual importance. Many devotees visit this temple to seek blessings and witness the unique self-manifested idol of Lord Ganesha.

History

The Kanipakam temple was built during the 11th century by the Chola king Kulothunga Chola I and later expanded by the Vijayanagara kings. The temple holds great significance due to the self-emerging idol of Lord Ganesha, which is believed to grow in size over time.
